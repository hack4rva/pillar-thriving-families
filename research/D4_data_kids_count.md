## Limitations & "Unknown" Data

While the KIDS COUNT Data Center is highly comprehensive, users should note certain limitations:
* **Data Lag:** Due to the time required for national data collection and verification, most indicators in the annual Data Book reflect data from 1-2 years prior (e.g., the 2025 Data Book primarily reports 2023 data) [1].
* **Consolidated Missing Data:** In instances where specific demographic or localized data points are unavailable (such as certain metrics for U.S. territories), they have been standardized. **Data not reported for specific indicators in the current reporting year are classified uniformly as unavailable rather than repeating "Unknown" across datasets.**
