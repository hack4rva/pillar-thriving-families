Services Landscape C1 — Richmond Youth Workforce Programs

Research the current landscape of youth workforce programs in Richmond, Virginia. Focus on programs that serve teens ages 14–18 and provide employment, internship, job shadowing, or career readiness experiences.

For each program you can identify: document the operating organization, program type (summer job, year-round, internship, job shadowing, pre-employment training), age eligibility, application window or timing, location or service area, and any public contact information or website.

- Girls For A Change
- Boys & Girls Club of Metro Richmond
- Richmond Education Fund / YouthWork Richmond
- Any additional programs discoverable from public sources
