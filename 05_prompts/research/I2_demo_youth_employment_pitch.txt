Demo Strategy I2 — Youth Employment Demo and Pitch Guide

Develop a specific demo script and pitch guide for a Richmond youth employment pathway tool. The audience is hackathon judges who understand civic tech, may include City representatives, and will evaluate clarity, credibility, and realistic impact.

The demo should: open with a specific Richmond teen user scenario (not a statistic), show a believable 4–5 step tool walkthrough, explain what makes the content credible (sources, links to official guidance), name a real partner organization that could continue the work, and address the data limitation (no pre-existing dataset) honestly and confidently.

Cover: how to handle judge skepticism about data freshness, how to explain the privacy design, and how to make the continuation case without overclaiming.

Output
- Opening hook (30–45 seconds)
- Tool walkthrough script (2–3 minutes)
- Credibility and limits statement (30 seconds)
- Continuation pitch (30 seconds)
- Anticipated judge questions and responses
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
