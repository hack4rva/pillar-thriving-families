Opportunities F1 — Ranked Opportunity Spaces for Thriving Families

Synthesize research from across the Thriving Families problem landscape to produce a ranked list of opportunity spaces for civic tech teams. Rank by: clarity of user need, data tractability in 48 hours, demo credibility, realistic impact, and safety (low harm potential).

Include opportunities in both youth employment and maternal health. For each opportunity: describe the user, the unmet need, the simplest technology approach, the data required, and the risk level.

Rank from most to least recommended for a weekend hackathon team.

Output
- Ranked opportunity list (1 = most recommended) with rationale
- Data and skill requirements per opportunity
- Risk assessment per opportunity
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
