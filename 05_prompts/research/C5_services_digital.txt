Services Landscape C5 — Digital Service Delivery for Youth and Health in Richmond

Investigate the current digital touchpoints for youth workforce and health service delivery in Richmond. Focus on what exists today: websites, apps, portals, and online tools used by teens seeking employment, by employers hiring youth, and by health organizations accessing data.

For youth employment: what web presence do the major programs have? Is there a mobile app? Can teens apply online? Is there a single place that aggregates opportunities?

For maternal health: what digital tools do Richmond health organizations currently use to access VDH, ACS, and other public data? Do any organizations use dashboards or shared workspaces?

Identify where digital delivery is working and where it is absent or inadequate.

Output
- Digital touchpoints inventory for each problem area
- Assessment of gaps in digital coverage
- Inferences about user digital experience (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
