## 2️⃣ Investment Thesis

| Pillar | Core Idea | Example Companies |
|---|---|---|
| **Scientific Breakthrough** | Back founders who have generated peer-reviewed results or patents that open new market categories. | *DeepCell* (single-cell analysis), *HelioGen* (next-gen solar) |

## 5️⃣ Risks & Caveats

* **Capital Concentration** – Pillar VC focuses primarily on deep-tech; investors should be aware that exit timelines may extend beyond the typical 5-year horizon for SaaS-only startups.
* **Founder-CEO Overlap** – While co-ownership can accelerate growth, it may also lead to **decision-making bottlenecks** if the founding CEOs are stretched across multiple portfolio boards.
* **Market-Readiness** – Some scientific breakthroughs require **regulatory clearance** (e.g., FDA, EMA). Early-stage founders must budget for lengthy approval processes, which can strain cash-flow before a Series A round.
