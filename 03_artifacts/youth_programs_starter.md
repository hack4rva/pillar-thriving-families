## Virginia Work Permit Summary

Relevant for any Youth Employment Navigator tool that guides teens through work authorization.

**Source:** https://doli.virginia.gov/labor-law/youth-employment/ (verified 2026-03-18)

| Age Group | Work Permit Required? | Process |
|-----------|----------------------|---------|
| 14–15 | YES — Employment Certificate required | Electronic system (Virginia Electronic Employment Certificate System) OR paper forms; reviewed within 24 hours |
| 16–17 | NO permit required | Subject to hour/type-of-work restrictions, but no certificate needed |
| 18+ | No youth employment restrictions | Standard adult employment rules apply |

**Electronic system:** Three-party registration — youth, employer, and parent/guardian each register.

**Paper process:** Submit "Permission to Employ" form (parent/guardian) + "Employer Intent to Employ" form (employer) to:
DOLI Child Labor Division, 1570 Parham Road, Richmond, VA 23228
Phone: (804) 371-3104 ext. 242
